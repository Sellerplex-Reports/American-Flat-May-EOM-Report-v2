Strategic Summary

How DSP Contributed to Business Outcomes in May 2026

■ Retargeting Performance: May 2026 was a pure retargeting ramp — all DSP effort focused on warming existing audiences already engaged with the brand. The 84.1% NTB rate reflects the quality of these audiences, not broad prospecting reach.


■ Conversion Rate Lift: DSP-touched journeys convert at 4.20% vs. 1.70% for SA-only — a 2.46× improvement. When DSP fires first (DSP→SA), conversion rates spike to 10–16%.


■ Audience Amplification: Ad-exposed users generated 7.1× more revenue. Multi-frequency exposure drove 82.3% of total DSP-attributed sales.


■ Ramp Context: With only 17 active days, all metrics reflect an early learning phase. As retargeting pools mature and frequency builds through Q3, ROAS and conversion rates are expected to improve. Net-new audience prospecting is not yet in scope.

📐 LTV Methodology: 1-Year LTV = AOV × avg purchase frequency (1.646 orders/yr, from RFM dataset) — the expected annual revenue per customer. Running LTV Sum simply adds each year's 1-Yr LTV to the prior year's total (e.g., Year 3 = Year 1 LTV + Year 2 LTV + Year 3 LTV). This shows the total expected value of a customer acquired in Year 1 if retained across all years — a rough multi-year revenue projection per customer, not a discounted NPV. Est.
